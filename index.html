About US

A young Indian football enthusiast who was driven to educate as many Indians as possible towards Football in India founded Footy India on April 30, 2020. Footy India was first launched only on Instagram. We later launched a Twitter account, which was followed by a YouTube channel, then a website. Currently, we have more than 7,000 Instagram followers, 500 Twitter followers, and 1500 YouTube subscribers. We frequently post updates regarding the Men's and Women's national Team. We also cover news on the ISL, I-League, Durand Cup, and other competitions in which Indian Clubs compete!
